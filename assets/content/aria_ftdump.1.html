Use: aria_ft_dump <table_name> <index_num>

       -?, -h, --help
              Display help and exit.

       -c, --count
              Calculate per-word stats (counts and global weights).

       -d, --dump
              Dump index (incl. data offsets and word weights).

       -l, --length
              Report length distribution.

       -s, --stats
              Report global stats.

       -v, --verbose
              Be verbose.
